I am a post-doctoral Reseracher at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Sunayana Sitaram. I am interested in working on problems that focus on fair and equitable AI. And enabling LLMs with Knowlege graphs.

I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers in the past. I did my PhD thesis at IIT Delhi where I was advised by Prof. Mausam and Prof Soumen Chakrabarty. Prior to joining IITD, I have completed my Master's in Privacy and Security in Online Social Networks at the Department of Computer Science - IIIT Delhi, where I worked with Dr Ponnurangam Kumaraguru.
