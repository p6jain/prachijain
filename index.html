I am a post-doctoral Reseracher at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Sunayana Sitaram. I am interested in working on problems that focus on fair and equitable AI. And enabling LLMs with Knowlege graphs.

I have been fortunate to work with an amazing set of researchers in the past. I did my PhD thesis at IIT Delhi where I was advised by Prof. Mausam and Prof Soumen Chakrabarty. I also interned at University College London with Emine Yilmaz and Rishabh Mehrotra. Prior to my time in research, I spent two summers with Google Summer of Code working for Julia Language and Mozilla.

I graduated with a B.Tech (Hons.) in Electronics and Instrumentation from BITS Pilani, Goa, India in 2019. For more details, check my CV or hit me up on my email.
